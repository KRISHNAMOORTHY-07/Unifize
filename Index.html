On Unifize, we unify processes and the conversations that drive them, drastically reducing the time and headaches for achieving quality outcomes. You can also manage all the same processes you would find in an eQMS or quality module on an ERP system, including Non-Conformances, Root Cause Analyses, Corrective Actions, Internal Audits, Inspections etc.
Full Users have access to the entire application — dashboards, reports, create, update and collaborate on all process records. Limited Users are generally assigned to customers, vendors or other collaborators who need restricted access. Free Users can be invited to individual conversations to collaborate on via email or a lite version of the app. There are no license costs for Unifize Lite Users.
